About Art of Cocoa

A legacy of excellence in premium wholesale confectionery and corporate gifting since 2015

Your Premier B2B Confectionery Partner

Art of Cocoa stands as a beacon of quality and sophistication in the world of premium chocolates and confectionery. With over a decade of expertise, we've cultivated relationships with the finest producers worldwide to bring exceptional products to discerning B2B clients.

Specializing in bulk supply to corporates, supermarkets, hotels, and event organizers, we understand that every business requirement is unique. Our portfolio ranges from everyday indulgences to luxury corporate gifts that make lasting impressions.

Our commitment extends beyond supply - we craft lasting partnerships through reliability, quality assurance, and the artistry of fine confectionery.

Arvind Patel

Founder & Managing Director

With a vision to bring world-class confectionery to the Indian market, Arvind Patel established Art of Cocoa in 2015. His commitment to quality and customer relationships has built a legacy trusted by corporates nationwide.
